Nirvana

What is Nirvana?

A rock band that started from 1987, they made some big hits such as the infamous "Smells Like Teen Spirit", "Lithium", & "Come As You Are".

Nirvana was generally made up of 3 people, those being Kurt Cobain; the lead singer & guitarist, Krist Novoselic; the bassist, and many drummers before offically hiring Dave Grohl to take the role of drummer.

Sadly however, on 1994; Nirvana was forced to break apart due to the unfortunate fact that Kurt Cobain would meet a rather unexpected demise
